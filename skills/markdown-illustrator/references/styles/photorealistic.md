# photorealistic

Cinematic photorealistic photography — a Gen-AI exclusive style that SVG/Mermaid cannot achieve.

## Design Aesthetic

Cinematic photorealistic photography with dramatic lighting, shallow depth of field, and atmospheric mood. Film-quality visual storytelling that feels like a professional editorial or documentary photograph. Emphasizes natural textures, volumetric light, and environmental storytelling.

## Background

- Color: Contextual Environment (scene-dependent)
- Texture: Real-world materials — concrete, wood grain, fabric, atmospheric haze

## Visual Elements

- Shallow depth of field bokeh
- Volumetric/atmospheric lighting (golden hour, overcast, neon)
- Natural material textures and surfaces
- Cinematic aspect ratios
- Environmental context and props
- Realistic shadow and reflection
- Lens flare and chromatic aberration (subtle)
- Film grain (subtle)

## Style Rules

### Do

- Specify exact camera and lens (e.g. 'Sony A7IV, 85mm f/1.4')
- Declare light source direction and quality
- Use specific material descriptions ('weathered oak', 'brushed aluminum')
- Keep compositions clean with clear subject
- Match color temperature to article palette

### Don't

- Expect accurate text/lettering in generated images
- Request specific recognizable faces or likenesses
- Combine with flat/vector/cartoon elements
- Use as evidence for factual claims (use Path 4 Real Media instead)
- Overcrowd the scene with too many subjects

## Best For

Product hero shots, editorial lifestyle imagery, atmospheric concept visualization, architectural and interior scenes, food photography, nature and landscape, cinematic mood illustrations
