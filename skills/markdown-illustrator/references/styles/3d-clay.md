# 3d-clay

3D clay render / claymation style — a Gen-AI exclusive style popular for modern tech and youth-oriented content.

## Design Aesthetic

Adorable 3D claymation with soft matte materials, rounded edges, and warm diffused lighting. Miniature diorama feel with isometric or slightly elevated camera angle. Playful, tactile, and inviting — like handcrafted stop-motion characters and props rendered in high-fidelity 3D.

## Background

- Color: Soft Gradient (#F0F4F8 to #E2E8F0) or Solid Pastel
- Texture: Smooth matte clay surface with subtle fingerprint-like imperfections

## Visual Elements

- Rounded blob-like shapes with no sharp edges
- Soft ambient occlusion shadows
- Matte/satin material finish (no gloss or metallic)
- Isometric or slightly elevated 3/4 view
- Miniature diorama/tilt-shift scale feeling
- Subtle fingerprint impressions on clay surfaces
- Warm diffused studio lighting (no harsh shadows)
- Simple expressive character faces

## Style Rules

### Do

- Keep all edges rounded and organic
- Use soft diffused lighting from above
- Maintain consistent clay material across all elements
- Use isometric or macro camera angle
- Keep scenes simple with 1-3 focal elements

### Don't

- Add metallic/glass/glossy materials
- Use sharp geometric edges or hard corners
- Create complex multi-layered backgrounds
- Add text or labels onto clay surfaces
- Mix with flat 2D or photorealistic elements

## Best For

Tech product concept illustrations, startup and SaaS feature explanations, educational content for young audiences, app onboarding and tutorial visuals, playful brand storytelling, social media content cards
