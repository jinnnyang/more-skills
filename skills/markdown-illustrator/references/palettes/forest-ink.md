# forest-ink

Deep green-black ink on warm sage paper for outdoor, nature, and sustainability topics

## Background

- Color: Warm Sage Paper (#F5F1E8)
- Texture: Subtle green-tinted paper grain

## Colors

| Role | Color | Hex | Usage |
|------|-------|-----|-------|
| Background | Sage Paper | #F5F1E8 | Primary background canvas |
| Primary | Forest Black | #16251B | Main text, lines, display headers, structures |
| Muted | Moss Green | #5D665D | Secondary text, annotations, minor labels |
| Accent | Forest Green | #2E6B4F | Key semantic markers, numbers, active borders |
| Soft Fill | Soft Sage | #E8DFCF | Card backgrounds, section highlights, fills |

## Accent

Forest Green (#2E6B4F) for key semantic highlights. Use to highlight hiking routes, botanical elements, or sustainability key metrics.

## Semantic Constraint

Deep green-black ink on warm sage base. Restrained color usage — keep to moss green and soft sage fills. Accent forest green sparingly for key emphasis only. Do NOT render color names, hex codes, or role labels as visible text in the image.

## Compatible With

- `editorial-magazine` (primary, default pairing)
- `nature`

## Not Recommended With

- `swiss-international` (requires clean off-white backgrounds and high-saturation single accent colors)
- `neon` (dark theme only)

## Best For

Hiking guides, outdoor notes, sustainability essays, personal field notes, and grounded lifestyle blogs
