# ink-classic

Classic ink black on warm paper base for neutral, professional content

## Background

- Color: Warm Paper (#F3F0E8)
- Texture: Subtle warm paper grain

## Colors

| Role | Color | Hex | Usage |
|------|-------|-----|-------|
| Background | Warm Paper | #F3F0E8 | Primary background canvas |
| Primary | Ink Black | #0A0A0B | Main text, lines, display headers, structures |
| Muted | Warm Charcoal | #68625A | Secondary text, annotations, minor labels |
| Accent | Pure Black | #111111 | Key semantic markers, numbers, active borders |
| Soft Fill | Soft Paper | #EBE6DA | Card backgrounds, section highlights, fills |

## Accent

Pure Black (#111111) for key semantic highlights. Use sparingly to guide the reader's eye to high-priority numbers, section highlights, or active borders.

## Semantic Constraint

Classic ink black on warm paper base. Restrained color usage — keep to warm charcoal and soft paper grays. Accent pure black sparingly for key emphasis only. Do NOT render color names, hex codes, or role labels as visible text in the image.

## Compatible With

- `editorial-magazine` (primary, default pairing)
- `minimal` (warm variation)

## Not Recommended With

- `swiss-international` (requires clean off-white backgrounds and high-saturation single accent colors)
- `neon` (dark theme only)

## Best For

Business commentary, technology essays, product post-mortems, neutral editorial columns, and educational summaries
