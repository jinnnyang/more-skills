# ikb-blue

International Klein Blue accent on off-white and charcoal for technological, data, or engineering frameworks

## Background

- Color: Matte Off-White (#FAFAF8)
- Texture: Clean off-white matte texture, no paper grain

## Colors

| Role | Color | Hex | Usage |
|------|-------|-----|-------|
| Background | Matte Off-White | #FAFAF8 | Primary background canvas |
| Primary Text | Clean Black | #0A0A0A | Main text, outlines, grid labels |
| Accent | Klein Blue | #002FA7 | Key semantic highlight, focus card background |
| Accent Text | White | #FFFFFF | Text rendered on top of Accent card fill |
| Muted | Grid Gray | #737373 | Minor labels, annotations, captions |
| Soft Fill | Light Gray | #F0F0EE | Neutral cards, data bars, structures |
| Hairline | Border Gray | #D4D4D2 | Grid lines, cell dividers |

## Accent

International Klein Blue (#002FA7) is the singular high-saturation accent color. Use only for primary focus areas: a key metric, an action card, or a critical flow node.

## Semantic Constraint

Strict single-accent rules. White text must be used on IKB backgrounds for readability. No gradients or drop shadows. Do NOT render color names, hex codes, or role labels as visible text in the image.

## Compatible With

- `swiss-international` (primary, default pairing)
- `minimal`

## Not Recommended With

- `editorial-magazine` (conflicts with serif print and warm paper aesthetics)
- `vintage` / `retro` / `watercolor` (requires textured canvases and softer color transitions)
