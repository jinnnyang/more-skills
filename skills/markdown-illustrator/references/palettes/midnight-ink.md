# midnight-ink

Warm gilt gold and paper cream on midnight black background for cinematic, dramatic, or game-art content

## Background

- Color: Midnight Black (#0E0D0C)
- Texture: Fine dark paper grain

## Colors

| Role | Color | Hex | Usage |
|------|-------|-----|-------|
| Background | Midnight Black | #0E0D0C | Primary background canvas |
| Primary Text | Paper Cream | #ECE2CF | Main text, lines, display headers, structures |
| Muted | Warm Gilt | #9A8C75 | Secondary text, annotations, minor labels |
| Accent | Golden Amber | #D4A04A | Key semantic markers, numbers, active borders |
| Soft Fill | Dark Charcoal | #1A1714 | Card backgrounds, section highlights, fills |

## Accent

Golden Amber (#D4A04A) for key semantic highlights. Contrast of gold on dark background creates a premium, cinematic focus.

## Semantic Constraint

Warm gilt and paper cream on midnight black. No flat color blocks. Use soft gold accents to highlight high-value topics or key terms. Do NOT render color names, hex codes, or role labels as visible text in the image.

## Compatible With

- `editorial-magazine` (primary, default dark pairing)
- `fantasy-animation` (as a dark aesthetic variation)

## Not Recommended With

- `swiss-international` (requires clean off-white backgrounds)
- `minimal` (usually works best on light backgrounds)

## Best For

Game key art explainers, night-time street photography, cinematic analyses, dark-themed cultural reviews, and historical dramas
