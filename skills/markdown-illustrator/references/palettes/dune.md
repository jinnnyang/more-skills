# dune

Sandy gold palette on warm straw paper for design, aesthetics, and minimalist portfolios

## Background

- Color: Warm Straw (#F0E6D2)
- Texture: Sand paper texture

## Colors

| Role | Color | Hex | Usage |
|------|-------|-----|-------|
| Background | Warm Straw | #F0E6D2 | Primary background canvas |
| Primary | Charcoal Brown | #1F1A14 | Main text, lines, display headers, structures |
| Muted | Dune Clay | #6F6557 | Secondary text, annotations, minor labels |
| Accent | Desert Gold | #8F7650 | Key semantic markers, numbers, active borders |
| Soft Fill | Soft Dune | #D4C2A4 | Card backgrounds, section highlights, fills |

## Accent

Desert Gold (#8F7650) for key semantic highlights. Use to highlight designer names, premium details, or architectural tags.

## Semantic Constraint

Sandy gold palette on warm straw base. Restrained color usage — keep to dune clay and soft dune fills. Accent desert gold sparingly for key emphasis only. Do NOT render color names, hex codes, or role labels as visible text in the image.

## Compatible With

- `editorial-magazine` (primary, default pairing)
- `elegant`
- `minimal`

## Not Recommended With

- `swiss-international` (requires clean off-white backgrounds and high-saturation single accent colors)
- `neon` (dark theme only)

## Best For

Industrial design showcases, architectural studies, portfolio covers, art reviews, and high-fashion editorial posts
