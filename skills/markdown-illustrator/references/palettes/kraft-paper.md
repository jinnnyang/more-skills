# kraft-paper

Warm earth brown ink on kraft brown paper for nostalgic, artisan, and warm personal writing

## Background

- Color: Kraft Brown (#EEDFC7)
- Texture: Heavy fibrous kraft paper texture

## Colors

| Role | Color | Hex | Usage |
|------|-------|-----|-------|
| Background | Kraft Brown | #EEDFC7 | Primary background canvas |
| Primary | Deep Earth | #2A1E13 | Main text, lines, display headers, structures |
| Muted | Warm Cocoa | #755F49 | Secondary text, annotations, minor labels |
| Accent | Terracotta Orange | #9B5A2E | Key semantic markers, numbers, active borders |
| Soft Fill | Soft Tan | #DFC9A8 | Card backgrounds, section highlights, fills |

## Accent

Terracotta Orange (#9B5A2E) for key semantic highlights. Use to highlight dates, vintage details, or artisan tags.

## Semantic Constraint

Warm earth brown ink on kraft brown base. Restrained color usage — keep to warm cocoa and soft tan fills. Accent terracotta orange sparingly for key emphasis only. Do NOT render color names, hex codes, or role labels as visible text in the image.

## Compatible With

- `editorial-magazine` (primary, default pairing)
- `vintage`
- `retro`
- `sketch-notes`

## Not Recommended With

- `swiss-international` (requires clean off-white backgrounds and high-saturation single accent colors)
- `neon` (dark theme only)

## Best For

Nostalgic essays, old object studies, creator notes, personal journals, and low-tech/artisan topics
