# Verified Artifacts Registry

Use this registry to store ONLY trusted, cross-verified multimedia assets (images, charts, videos) harvested from highly credible sources (official domains, government sites, peer-reviewed journals).

> [!CAUTION]
> **Accessibility (a11y) Rules for Alt-Text**:
> The `alt` text MUST provide a complete visual substitution for blind users. Do not just write "Chart of sales". Write: "A bar chart showing 2023 sales peaking in Q4 at $1.2M, double the Q1 amount. X-axis shows quarters, Y-axis shows revenue in USD."

## Formatting Rule
Each entry must be exactly one line in the format:
`[Global Citation Number]: ![Detailed Accessibility Alt-Text](https://trusted-url.com/image.png "Short Title")`
